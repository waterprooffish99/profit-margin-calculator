## 🛠️ Key Technical Implementations

### 1. Smart Paywall Logic (`src/components/Calculator.tsx`)
- **Limit:** 5 unique product calculations.
- **Unique Product Hashing:** To prevent "premature triggers," the app only counts a "try" when a new combination of **Price + COGS** is entered.
- **Debounce:** A 10-second stability timer ensures users aren't penalized while typing.
- **Owner Bypass:** Use `Alt + Click` on the "Limit Reached!" heading to reset the `localStorage` counter for testing.

### 2. Monetization & Payment (`src/constants.ts`)
- **Gumroad Link:** `https://salmanhasssan.gumroad.com/l/profit-margin-calculator`
- **Pricing Strategy:** $19 Lifetime Access (One-time payment).
- **Payment Bridge:** Configured for international payments via Payoneer virtual USD accounts.

### 3. Value-Add UI Enhancements
- **Feature Locking:** "Copy Summary" and "Download CSV" buttons are locked for free users once they reach 4 tries. Clicking them redirects to the payment page.
- **Offer Stacking:** The Paywall Modal now highlights a "Bonus Bundle" (Checklist & Strategy Guide) to increase perceived value.
