## Feature 4 — Reading Your Results
**What the colors mean:**
- 🟢 **Green** = Excellent. You are making a very high profit margin.
- 🟡 **Amber** = Good. You are in a healthy, sustainable profit range.
- 🔴 **Red** = Danger. Your profit margins are too low, and you might be losing money after fees.

**What each number means:**
- **Net Profit:** The exact amount of cash you keep in your pocket after subtracting the product cost, shipping cost, and platform fees from your selling price.
- **Gross Margin %:** The percentage of the selling price that is profit. If you sell for $100 and keep $40, your margin is 40%.
- **Markup %:** How much you marked up the product from its original cost. If a product costs $50 and you sell it for $100, your markup is 100%.
