## 🏗️ 1. DEPLOYMENT (Getting your Live Link)
To show people the tool, it needs to be online. I recommend **Vercel** because it's free and ultra-fast.

1.  **Sign in to [Vercel.com](https://vercel.com)** using your GitHub account.
2.  Click **"Add New"** > **"Project"**.
3.  Find your `profit-margin-calculator` repository and click **"Import"**.
4.  Keep all settings as default (Vercel automatically detects Vite).
5.  Click **"Deploy"**.
6.  **Success!** You will get a URL like `profit-margin-calculator.vercel.app`. **This is your sales link.**
